Call for Videos for the KR 2025 Video Track

Following the success of the first edition of KR's video track, we are excited to launch its second edition, which will be held at the 22nd International Conference on Principles of Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KR 2025). The call for submissions is now open!

Goals

  • Boost KR's reach: Help researchers share their work in ways that a wider public can enjoy and understand.
  • Spotlight interesting applications and insights: Give KR work a stage, whether it's breakthroughs for science, industry, or just curious minds!
  • Sharpen your science communication skills: Challenge your ability to explain complex KR concepts with clarity, creativity, and confidence, whether to experts or a general audience.

Two videos will receive special recognition: the Best Video Award (selected by the PC) and the Public Choice Award (decided by KR 2025 participants). The winners will be announced at the KR 2025 conference. All accepted videos will be published on the official KR Conference YouTube channel.

Video authors of accepted videos are not obliged to register for the KR 2025 conference. However, we would be delighted to congratulate authors of award-winning videos at the KR 2025 award ceremony in person.

Topics of Interest

The topic of the video can be from all subfields of knowledge representation and reasoning. We encourage submissions that can serve as educational resources or make KR results, projects, or software more approachable for a broader audience.

Possible video topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Introduction to a particular research area or a research project relevant to KR.
  • Visualisation of methods or research results relevant to KR.
  • Introduction of non-commercial software tools developed for KR or relevant for KR researchers.

For examples and inspiration, please check the last year videos: KR 2024 Video Track.

Technical Requirements for the Video

  • Video must be relevant to KR. If in doubt, please consult the calls for KR 2025 main or special tracks, or email us at kr2025-video@easychair.org.
  • Videos must be in English or include English subtitles.
  • Video length should be 3 - 15 minutes.
  • Video format: AVI or MPEG4.
  • Videos must adhere to copyright laws.

Submission Information

A submission to the video track consists of two items:

  1. The link to the video.
  2. A one-page document with:
    • Names, affiliations, and email addresses of video authors.
    • Title of the video.
    • Brief abstract describing the video's content (from one sentence to one paragraph).
    • Description of the targeted audience: PhD, undergraduate/post-graduate students, high-school students, or the general public.

Submit via EasyChair by selecting the KR Video Track: https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=kr2025.

Evaluation Criteria for Acceptance of the Video

  • Presentation quality: The video makes good use of the visual medium (static, one-shot recordings of talks are discouraged).
  • Educational value: Is the material conducive to learning and understanding?
  • Soundness: Are the facts consistent with existing research?
  • Suitability for target audience: Is the presentation style appropriate?
  • Entertainment value: Is it enjoyable to watch?

Important Dates

The deadlines are AoE - Anywhere on Earth (UTC-12).

  • Video submission deadline: July 24, 2025
  • Notification of acceptance: August 16, 2025

Inquiries

If you have questions regarding the video track, please email the Video Track Chairs at kr2025-video@easychair.org.
