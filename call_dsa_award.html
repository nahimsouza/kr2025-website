KR Distinguished Service Award: Call for Nominations

The KR Distinguished Service Award serves to recognize exceptional and sustained service to the Knowledge Representation and Reasoning community, whether it be service to the KR conference series, the KR Inc. organization, other KRR-related events, or more generally, any (non-research) activities that have helped foster a vibrant research community and/or increase the visibility of the KRR field.

Nominations

The Awards Committee encourages KRR researchers to nominate individuals they feel are deserving of the Distinguished Service Award.

Nominations should be submitted via EasyChair (select the "KR Distinguished Award" track): https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=kr2025

Please put the name of the nominee in the Title box and upload a single PDF with a short text (1-2 paragraphs) describing the nominee's services to the community, as well as a brief CV. Any text can be added to the Abstract and Keywords boxes.

Important Dates

  • Nomination deadline: May 20, 2025 (AoE time)
  • Award winner notification: No later than June 30, 2025

The official announcement will be made at KR 2025
