Diversity and Inclusion Grants

Following the KR Inc. D&I policy, there will be funding available to provide grants to attend KR 2025. These grants will come with a registration fee waiver and partial funding for travel costs. Those who lack the means to pay for registration or who cannot afford to travel to attend the conference may apply for financial support by filling in the form until September 1, 2025.

The grants are aimed at attendees (Postdocs, PhD students, researchers, academics) from underrepresented groups and countries in KR.

We are planning on selecting applicants based on wide diversity and inclusion criteria.

We are committed to fostering an inclusive community with opportunities for all participants without regard to age, religion, race, ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, or disability.

Please also note that people who apply for a grant should wait for the notification on September 5, 2025 before registering.

Important Dates

The deadlines are AoE - Anywhere on Earth (UTC-12).

  • Application Deadline: September 1, 2025
  • Notification: September 5, 2025
