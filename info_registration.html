Information for Authors

At least one author of each paper accepted to the main conference program must register, with payment of either the regular or student fee. The authors are responsible for deciding who will register and present the paper.

Please note that in-person attendance and presentation are mandatory for all accepted papers. Remote or online presentations will not be permitted.

ICAPS colocation

If you are registering for both ICAPS and KR, we can provide a discount of $300 AUD for the second registration. Please register for ICAPS first, then submit your registration to the KR registration team (events@deakin.edu.au) and they will issue a discount code for you.

Confirmation & Invitation Letter

You will receive email confirmation after completing the online registration form at the top of the page.

Payment

Payments can be made online via credit card (VISA or Mastercard only).

Cancellation Policy & Refund

For cancellation requests received on or before September 30, 2025, the registration fee will be refunded less an administration charge of $100 AUD.

For cancellation requests received between October 1 and October 31, the registration fee will be refunded 50% less an administration charge of $100 AUD.

Registration fees will not be refunded in any portion if cancellation is received after October 31.

Questions?

Reach out to events@deakin.edu.au for any questions regarding registration. Make sure to include KR2025 in the subject.
