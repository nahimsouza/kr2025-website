Student Grants

A very limited number of student grants for attending KR 2025 are available.

Grants will be given to students who are attending the conference and lack sufficient funding. Preference will be given to students who are going to present a paper and/or were selected for the Doctoral Consortium at KR 2025.

Important Dates

The deadlines are AoE - Anywhere on Earth (UTC-12).

  • Application Deadline: September 1, 2025
  • Notification: September 5, 2025

Application

Applications for student grants can be submitted using the following link: https://forms.gle/etgWkZWrfEYtutYS8.
